Standard Terms and Conditions Detail

MATERIALS: All materials will be ordered upon contract execution and submittal approval. Material and handling charges are billed in full upon receipt and are not subject to retainage. An 8% late fee applies to material invoices not paid within 30 days. Materials may be stored offsite by LGHVAC Mechanical, LLC with proof of insurance provided to the Purchaser, or delivered to the jobsite and stored at the Purchaser's responsibility and expense.

PAYMENT TERMS: Payment terms are Net 40 from date of pay application submission. Monthly invoices will reflect all equipment, materials, labor, supervision, and applicable overhead and profit as outlined in this proposal. A service charge of 1.5% per month will apply to balances outstanding beyond 40 days. In the event of non-payment requiring collection efforts, the Purchaser agrees to cover reasonable attorney's fees and all associated collection costs.

RETAINAGE: Retainage shall not exceed 10% and may only be withheld on the labor portion of the contract. Retainage will be released upon issuance of the Certificate of Occupancy. For multi-building projects, retainage shall be released on a per-building basis upon completion of each building, not at the conclusion of the entire project.

DAMAGES OR BACK-CHARGES: Any damages or back-charges must be communicated to LGHVAC Mechanical, LLC in writing within thirty (30) days of occurrence. Failure to provide an itemized account within this period will relieve LGHVAC Mechanical, LLC of responsibility. Back-charges resulting from scheduling delays caused by other trades or plan revisions shall not be the responsibility of LGHVAC Mechanical, LLC.

WARRANTY: All work described in this proposal is covered by a one (1) year warranty beginning on the date of equipment startup or substantial completion, whichever occurs first. This warranty does not cover damage caused by misuse, unauthorized modifications, improper maintenance, or normal wear and tear. For multi-building projects, the warranty period applies individually to each building from its date of completion.

DURATION OF PROPOSAL: This proposal is valid for thirty (30) days from the date of issue and is subject to change or withdrawal by LGHVAC Mechanical, LLC at any time prior to acceptance.

CANCELLATION: If the Purchaser cancels all or any portion of an accepted proposal without cause or without written consent from LGHVAC Mechanical, LLC, the Purchaser will be responsible for all material and equipment cancellation charges, restocking fees, and any other project-related costs incurred to date. LGHVAC Mechanical, LLC will make every reasonable effort to return or cancel previously ordered materials to minimize these charges.

CONTRACT TYPE: All pricing is based on a contract with payment due within 40 days of pay application submission. If a pay-when-paid contract is required, LGHVAC Mechanical, LLC will submit a schedule of values equivalent to 60 days of progress billing, with material costs billed as Pay Application #2. Work will commence upon receipt of this initial payment to ensure the project is not financed by LGHVAC Mechanical, LLC on behalf of the Owner and General Contractor.
